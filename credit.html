CREDITS

What are Credits?

Whenever you play in any of our events, promote, post on social media, or blog about us you'll earn Credits based on how much you get involved. First, we have a list of tasks that will determine how many credits you’ll earn, which then will be added directly to your GGtoor credits account on your profile. We will be tracking every one of the posts and articles and tagging them any and automatically add to a player's credit account The Credits program builds upon task related features and makes them accessible to more players.

Nope! Also, The GGtoor Shop is here to stay, now open all year round. GGtoor Shop items are yours to keep and you can redeem your Credits for merchandise in our GGtoor Shop. You will see that the credits you will earn have a value of .01 of a USA dollar that means that $1.00 Equals 100 Credits. In this page, you will see a list of tasks with the number of Credits you can earn we will be tracking the task by tagging them once is done we will have a report in real-time of your earned credits. Visit the tab "Win Credits" to find tools.
